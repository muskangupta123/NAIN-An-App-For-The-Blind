NAIN - An App For the Blind

Empowering the visually impaired with advanced object detection.

Detect Objects

Additional Features

360-Degree Audio Navigation

Enhanced audio cues for immersive navigation, providing a 360-degree spatial awareness to the visually impaired.

Object Identification and Search

Advanced object detection and search capabilities to identify and locate objects in the surroundings.

Alert System

An intelligent alert system that notifies users about potential obstacles or hazards in their path.

Sign Board Reader

Real-time sign board recognition to assist users in reading and understanding information on signs.

General Voice Interaction

Natural language processing for voice interactions, making the app intuitive and easy to use through voice commands.
